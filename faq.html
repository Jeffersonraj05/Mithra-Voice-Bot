MITHRA - Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

What is MITRA?
MITRA is a voice assistant designed to help users navigate through metro stations, find routes, and access station information using natural language processing.
How do I change the language in MITRA?
You can change the language by clicking on the "Language" dropdown in the top-right corner. Choose between English, Tamil, or Hindi for your preferred language.
How can I use voice recognition with MITRA?
Simply click the microphone icon next to the "To" input field. When you speak, MITRA will recognize the station name and fill the input field automatically.
Is MITRA available on mobile devices?
Yes, MITRA is fully compatible with mobile devices, and you can use it both on desktop and mobile browsers for a seamless experience.
How accurate is the route information provided by MITRA?
MITRA uses up-to-date information to provide accurate routes. However, we recommend checking for any updates or disruptions in metro services for the most accurate travel details.
Can MITRA find the nearest metro station?
Yes, MITRA can find the nearest metro station based on your current location using geolocation services.
